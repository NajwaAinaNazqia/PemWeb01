Our Services

What We Do?

Repair

Like other electronic components, AC components also often experience damage. Examples of damage that often occur are the compressor not working, system leaks and so on

Trades-In

provides a service for exchanging your used AC with a new AC with various brands and the best quality,
so you no longer need to buy a new AC in a shop at an expensive price.

Installation of new Ac

Don't install the AC yourself if you are not an expert, we accept new AC installation services, with technicians who are professional and experienced in installing AC in various places.

Cleaning the AC

If the AC is not cleaned regularly, dirt, dust and even mold and bacteria that accumulate, for example in the ventilation or filter section,
will make the AC work less than optimally.
And this is what makes its use even more wasteful of electricity.

Replace AC Sparepart

Most people think that a home AC that is still cold is still in good condition and does not need maintenance. Even though that's not necessarily the case. In some cases, even though the AC remains cold, sometimes the AC components are damaged and need to be replaced.

Fill AC Freon

This AC component does have a very important role, the only condition where the AC needs to be refilled with freon is when the freon pipe is damaged and needs to be replaced. Therefore, in order for the AC to function normally again, the freon must be refilled.
